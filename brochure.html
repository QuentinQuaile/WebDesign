Examples of symbols for headstones

Bird

Birds are often a symbol of peace. When in flight they also may represent the winged soul, a tradition which goes back to Egyptian times. Sometimes the wings alone are chosen.

Fox

The fox is often a symbol of cunning and intelligence. In Finnish mythology, it symbolizes the victory of intelligence over both malevolence and brute strength. This fox was carved into a limestone headstone in sunken relief.

Snowdrops

The snowdrop is a symbol of hope. The above headstone carving in slate is very delicate and has been painted with a light wash of white and gold.
